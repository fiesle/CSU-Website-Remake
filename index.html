How to Enroll?

STEP 1

  Login with your username and password(the one you were initially given during the registration period last semester).

  In case of lost login details, click [Forgot Password] and fill up the necessary steps for retrieval.

STEP 2

  Click [enroll for 2nd semester].

STEP 3

  Read and understand the Data Privacy Consent and information on RA 10931.

  To Continue, click [Agree].

STEP 4

  Your information is then requested to be updated. You are also requested to select whether to [opt in/avail] or [opt out/not to avail] of RA 10931 for this semester.

  To continue, [Click update and proceed to enroll].
